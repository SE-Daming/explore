# Content Creation Guide

## Article Types

### 1. Tool Reviews
Deep-dive reviews of individual AI tools.

**Template:**
- Introduction (what is this tool)
- Key features breakdown
- Pricing analysis
- Pros & cons
- Who it's for
- Alternatives
- Final verdict & rating
- FAQ section

**Word count:** 1,500-3,000 words

### 2. Comparison Articles
Head-to-head comparisons of 2-3 tools.

**Template:**
- Quick overview of each tool
- Feature comparison table
- Pricing comparison
- Use case recommendations
- Final verdict
- CTA to try each tool

**Word count:** 1,500-2,500 words

### 3. "Best X Tools" Lists
Curated lists of top tools in a category.

**Template:**
- Introduction explaining criteria
- Quick comparison table
- Individual tool highlights (3-5 sentences each)
- Final recommendations
- CTA section

**Word count:** 2,000-3,000 words

## SEO Guidelines

### Target Keywords
- "Best AI [use case] tools 2024"
- "[Tool name] review"
- "[Tool A] vs [Tool B]"
- "AI tools for [specific task]"

### On-Page SEO
- Include keyword in title, H1, first paragraph
- Use H2/H3 for sections
- Add internal links to related tools
- Include external links to official tool sites
- Meta description: 150-160 characters

### Content Quality
- Use the tool yourself before reviewing
- Include real screenshots/examples
- Be honest about limitations
- Update reviews quarterly

## Affiliate Link Guidelines

### Disclosure
Always include affiliate disclosure at the top of reviews:
> "This article contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you make a purchase, at no extra cost to you."

### Link Placement
- Primary CTA after introduction
- CTA in conclusion
- CTA buttons should say "Try [Tool]" or "Get Started"
- Use `rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow"` on all affiliate links

## Writing Tips

1. **Hook in first paragraph** - Address pain point
2. **Use short paragraphs** - 2-3 sentences max
3. **Add visual breaks** - Tables, lists, CTAs
4. **Write for scanners** - Use subheadings
5. **Include numbers** - "10x faster", "saves 5 hours"
